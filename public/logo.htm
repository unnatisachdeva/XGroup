Frequently asked questions

What is the best background remover for product photos?

Photoroom is built specifically for e‑commerce and brand visuals. Our AI background remover instantly erases backgrounds, leaving a transparent backdrop that you can replace with a studio or lifestyle setting, if necessary.

How does an AI background remover work?

Upon uploading an image, Photoroom’s AI background remover detects and isolates the main subject of your image (a product, person, or object), separating it from the background before erasing it.

Can I remove the background from an image without losing quality?

With Photoroom, you can remove the background from any image while preserving every detail. Our AI background remover maintains image resolution, sharpness, and edge precision, so your product or subject remains crisp and natural.

Is there a free background remover that works on mobile and desktop?

Photoroom is available on mobile and desktop. You can get started for free to remove backgrounds, and when you're ready, upgrade to access more AI photo editing tools and high-resolution exports.

How can I automatically remove and replace a background with a new one?

Upload your photo to Photoroom, and our AI background remover automatically erases the background. Keep it transparent, or replace it with a professional studio setup, lifestyle scene, or custom backdrop with AI Backgrounds.
